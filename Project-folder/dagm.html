Digital Annual General Meeting Project

This project involved designing a user-friendly interface for a digital platform that facilitates annual general meetings (AGMs) for organizations. The goal was to create an intuitive and efficient experience for users to participate in AGMs remotely. The design focused on clear navigation, easy access to meeting materials, and interactive features to enhance engagement during the meetings.
